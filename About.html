About Me

I am Devansh Sharma.

Born in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, I have lived in Gurgaon, Kolkata, and Bengaluru—each city shaping my journey and experiences.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science (Cybersecurity) at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, driven by a passion for technology and digital security.

My family resides in Mumbai, where my father serves as the Vice President at Reliance Industries.

Beyond academics, I have been a guitarist for over 10 years, blending melody with my love for rock and metal music.

Fitness is an integral part of my life—I dedicate two hours daily to the gym, pushing limits and embracing discipline.

This is me—a creator, a problem solver, and a relentless learner.
